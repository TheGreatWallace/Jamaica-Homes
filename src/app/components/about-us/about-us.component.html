ABOUT US

Jamaica Homes

From an island of humble beginnings from once being ruled by slave masters to becoming world leaders on the global stage shows how resilient and ambitious our people are. Jamaica Homes was created in 2022 to allow for the readily availability of houses for rent all over the island. It was especially created for students who may have wanted to live in close proximity to their respective universities and colleges.
